KubeVoIP models a SIP platform as Kubernetes resources. The core resources are
`SIPGateway`, `MediaRelay`, `SIPUser`, `SIPTrunk`, `CallScope`, `DialPolicy`,
`CallRoute`, and `AsteriskPool`.
